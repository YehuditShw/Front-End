shoot-bubble/ Instructions

Goal of the game:
Blow up all the balls on the game fighter as fast as possible --- !!!

how do you play?
The only option to blow up balls and take them out of the game board

is to send them a friend of the same color-
As soon as they meet everyone will explode together ...
After each ball is sent a new ball of a color unknown in advance ....

pay attention!!
In 3 minutes you must finish emptying the entire game board--
The more balls you explode, the higher your score will be .. !!
To send the balls, aim with the right and left arrows on the keyboard
And send them up with the up arrow.
Pay attention to the timer that appears on the side of the game ...

Be focused, think of moves ... and make sure your explosions get bigger and bigger -
that way you will score more and more points.
Did you succeed ?!

We'll see you do it again more quickly -----
Come on ---- Enjoy !!!
